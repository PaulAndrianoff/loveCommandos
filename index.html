Is dowry still legal in India ?

YES
NO
Good answer.
The Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961 consolidated the anti-dowry laws which had been passed on certain states.This legislation provides for a penalty if any person gives, takes or abets giving or receiving of dowry. The punishment could be imprisonment for minimum 5 years and a fine more than ₹15,000 or the value of the dowry received, whichever is higher. Dowry in the Act is defined as any property or valuable security given or agreed to be given in connection with the marriage.
